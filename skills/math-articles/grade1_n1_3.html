N1.3: Solve operations using whole numbers

Let's look at the first five numbers:
1, 2, 3, 4, 5

We can do something called add these numbers. For example, 1 + 1 is 2 and 1 + 2 is 3.

{% include definition.html term="Add/Plus" definition="Combine two numbers to make a bigger number." %}

We can also subtract them. For example, 4 - 1 is 3, and 5 - 1 is 4.

{% include definition.html term="Subtract/Minus" definition="Combine two numbers to make a smaller number." %}

Here is the full list of additions:

Equation Answer
1 + 1 2
1 + 2 3
1 + 3 4
1 + 4 5
2 + 2 4
2 + 3 5
4 + 1 5
